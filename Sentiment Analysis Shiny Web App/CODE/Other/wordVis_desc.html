About Word Count Visualization

Before doing sentiment analysis, we tried to understand the structure of the data through word count visualization.

We tried to describe our text through the following visualizations:

1.Word count analysis: interactive tables with tokens in descending order of frequency.

2.Word count chart: bar chart showing frequency of top N tokens.

3.Word cloud: visual representation of frequency (or importance) of words in our dataset.

These visualizations help us understand what the keywords are and also the overall "theme" of our results.

(Note: When viewing our visualizations please allow a few seconds for it to render)
