About our Dataset

Our web app allows users to evaluate and analyze the sentiment of product reviews. We have used a static electronics product reviews dataset compiled by Julian McCauley of UCSD and Stanford University. This dataset has over a million reviews and is around 1.5 gigabytes in size. It covers a diverse range of electronics offered from different manufacturers and related information. The raw unprocessed dataset can be found at this URL: https://snap.stanford.edu/data/web-Amazon.html.

(Note: When viewing our visualizations please allow a few seconds for it to render)

Below are the attributes after cleaning and formatting the raw dataset:

1.asin: unique ID for the product

2.product_name: name of the product

3.reviewerID: unique ID for the reviewer

4.summary: short description of the review

5.reviewText: detailed description of the review

6.overall_ratings: overall rating (out of 5) given by a reviewer
